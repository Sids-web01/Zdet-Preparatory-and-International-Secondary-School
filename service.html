Our Services

Zadet International School offers a wide range of services designed to support the academic and personal development of our students. From advanced placement courses to extracurricular activities, we provide an enriching environment that fosters growth and success.

Academic Programs

Comprehensive JSS to SSS curriculum with Science, Arts, and Commercial tracks. Advanced placement courses available.

Sports & Recreation

Extensive sports programs including football, basketball, athletics, and indoor games. Professional coaching available.

Guidance & Counseling

Professional counseling services for academic guidance, career planning, and personal development support.

Technology Integration

Modern computer labs, digital classrooms, and technology-enhanced learning experiences for 21st-century skills.

Extracurricular Activities

Wide range of clubs and activities including debate, drama, music, art, and community service programs.

Library Services

Comprehensive library with extensive collection of books, digital resources, and quiet study spaces for research.
